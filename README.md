# Madheads Cart Share

Share your [Madheads Coffee](https://madheadscoffee.com) shopping cart with friends.

## How it works

1. Drag the **Share Cart** button to your bookmarks bar
2. On madheadscoffee.com, add items to your cart
3. Click the bookmarklet → link copied to clipboard
4. Send link to friends
5. Friends open the link and click the bookmarklet → items added to their cart

Multiple shared carts can be imported — quantities accumulate.
